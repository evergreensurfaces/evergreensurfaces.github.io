Our Story

A Company Built on Care & Craftsmanship

We started Evergreen Surface Solutions with a simple goal: give homeowners a team they can trust with the surfaces that matter most. Log homes especially demand patience, the right products, and proper preparation - and that’s exactly the work we love.

Whether we’re re-staining a full log home, restoring a weathered deck, or soft washing years of grime off your siding, we treat every property with the same respect: careful prep, premium materials, tidy work, and a finish designed to protect for years to come. We’re based in Lafayette and travel for log cabin work across multiple states.
